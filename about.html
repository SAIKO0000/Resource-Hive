ABOUT US

Resource Hive represents a significant step forward in resource management for CvSU's DIT. By providing a centralized platform, it simplifies the booking and allocation of rooms and equipment, saving valuable time for faculty and staff. With intuitive navigation and an efficient booking system, users can easily manage reservations for classrooms, labs, and other essential spaces. This not only reduces scheduling conflicts but also facilitates better planning, allowing educators and staff members to focus more on their primary responsibilities of teaching and research, rather than logistical concerns.
